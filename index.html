Mission Statement

In the time of need, we provide a second chance for wildlife to be able to thrive and succeed as nature had intended. We rescue, rehabilitate, and release wildlife. We are state/federal nonprofit 501 (c).

Who We Are

Here at the Safe Haven Wildlife Rehabilitation, we have a type C wildlife rehabilitaiton permit. This permits us to rehabilitate many different species of wildlife. We specialize in rehabilitating racoons, rabbits, squirrels, weasels, and minks. Although there are species we do not work with, please contact us with any questions regarding any wildlife. Our rehabbers are well equipped to help find any animal the care it needs!

Helen Svetlecich

Helen mainly rehabilitates squirrels and rabbits. Helen has been rehabbing wildlife for over thirteen years and prides herself on her great success rate!
